About the School

Senior High School within
Bacoor Elementary School

Senior High School within Bacoor Elementary School was established on 2016. It is one of the five public senior high schools at the Division of Bacoor City. It started catering students under the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Strand and later on the Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand on 2020.

Located in Tinocoro St., Brgy. Poblacion, City of Bacoor, Cavite — proudly serving the local community with quality public education. The school main gate is situated in Barangay Campo Santo, making it one of the key constituents and stakeholders of the community.

A quick look

Senior High School within Bacoor ES — a learning home for STEM and HUMSS campers, empowering minds and enriching futures.

Mission

To deliver quality, learner-centered Senior High School education that builds competent, values-driven, and future-ready campers.

Vision

A leading senior high community in Bacoor, nurturing well-rounded learners ready for higher education and life.

External Context

SHS within Bacoor ES was first housed in SHS Dulong Bayan. In June 2021, the school was transferred and housed within Bacoor ES. The school promotes educational growth in Barangay Campo Santo and helps uplift the standard of living and enhances participation in the basic functions of society.

Community & Political

The chairperson of the Local School Board is the City Mayor, where the schools share the Special Education Fund (SEF). The City Mayor has control over the Local Government Unit (LGU), which shares with the Provincial Government Unit (PGU) to support the schools.

Academic Track

STEM Strand

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

About the Strand

The STEM strand under the Academic Track prepares students for college courses in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and research skills essential for STEM-related degrees.

Core Subjects

  • Pre-Calculus & Basic Calculus
  • General Chemistry 1 & 2
  • General Biology 1 & 2
  • General Physics 1 & 2
  • Research / Capstone Project
  • Empowerment Technologies

Career Pathways

EngineeringMedicineArchitecture Computer ScienceNursingEducation (Science/Math) Information TechnologyEnvironmental Science

Year Offered

STEM has been offered at SHS within Bacoor ES since 2016, the founding year of the school.

Academic Track

HUMSS Strand

Humanities & Social Sciences

About the Strand

The HUMSS strand focuses on society, culture, politics, literature, and the arts. It develops students' communication, analytical, and creative skills — ideal for those pursuing careers in education, law, journalism, social work, and the humanities.

Core Subjects

  • Creative Writing
  • Trends, Networks & Critical Thinking
  • Introduction to World Religions & Belief Systems
  • Disciplines & Ideas in Social Sciences
  • Philippine Politics & Governance
  • Community Engagement & Solidarity
